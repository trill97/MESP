parsun

Parsun has patents in their own name and are the No1 exporter of Outboard motors from China (excluding Mercury plant in Suzhou, China). In 2013 Parsun increased their plant area to 56,0000 sq metres and increased production to 180,000 motors per year and has bold plans to extend their model range into the 200hp-300hp sector.
